Ship Names Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ship Names ("the App"), developed by Josh Larson ("Developer"), handles user information.

Data Collection

Ship Names does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data or user information.

  • No user accounts required
  • No personal information collected
  • No user tracking
  • No access to contacts, photos, location, or device identifiers
  • No data transmitted to external servers

All functionality operates entirely on the user's device.

Third-Party Services

Ship Names does not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or third-party data collection services.

App Store Privacy Disclosure

Developer collects data: No
Data linked to user: None
Tracking used: No

Children's Privacy

Ship Names does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13, and complies with COPPA requirements.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated in the future, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the App, please visit our contact page.
