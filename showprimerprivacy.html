ShowPrimer Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

ShowPrimer ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

Analytics

The App uses TelemetryDeck, a privacy-first analytics service, to collect anonymous usage data. This helps us understand how the App is used and improve it over time.

The following anonymous signals are collected:

  • Search queries performed
  • Setlist loading events (number of shows and songs found)
  • Playlist creation events (number of songs added)
  • API request performance (response status and timing)
  • API retry and rate-limiting events

TelemetryDeck does not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, or Apple ID. Analytics data cannot be used to identify you. For more details, see TelemetryDeck's privacy policy.

Apple Music

The App uses Apple's MusicKit framework to search the Apple Music catalog and create playlists in your library. Access to Apple Music requires your permission and is handled entirely by Apple's frameworks. The App does not access or store your Apple Music credentials.

Setlist Data

The App retrieves concert setlist information from the setlist.fm API. No personal information is sent to setlist.fm beyond the artist search queries you initiate.

Data Storage

The App does not maintain user accounts and does not store personal data on any server. All data remains on your device.

Third-Party Services

The App connects to the following third-party services:

Apple Music — catalog search and playlist creation
setlist.fm — concert setlist data
TelemetryDeck — anonymous analytics

Each service is governed by its own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the App, please visit our contact page.
